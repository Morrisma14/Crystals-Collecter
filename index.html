CrystalsCollector!

You Will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

Win the game by matching your total score to the random number! If your total score goes over the amount of the random number, you lose.

You must figure out the value of each crystal by clicking on it.

Each time the game starts, the valve of the crystals will reset.
